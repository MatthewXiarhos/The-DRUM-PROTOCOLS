The home page now speaks the same design language as the rest of the site.

Instead of the older image-tile landing page, this version uses the same restrained editorial look as the newer components: slate header fields, gold accents, serif display type, soft bordered cards, and clearer hierarchy.

Healing
Downshift safely
For over-stimulation, anxiety, distress, and recovery-oriented use cases where the nervous system needs less activation and more room.
Thriving
Hold what works
For stable cognitive or contemplative states such as focus, clarity, meditation, and creative work when the goal is steadiness.
Transforming
