Three series · one shared framework

Every protocol belongs to a direction of change.

The library is organized around three physiological directions — Healing, Thriving, and Transforming. Each series uses a distinct rhythmic architecture matched to the nervous system state it addresses. Select a series below to explore its protocols.

Healing
Downshift safely
For over-stimulation, anxiety, distress, and recovery-oriented states where the nervous system needs less activation and more room to settle.
Explore Healing protocols →
Thriving
Hold what works
For stable cognitive or contemplative states — focus, clarity, meditation, and creative work — where the goal is sustaining what is already working.
Explore Thriving protocols →
Transforming
Build upward
For low-affect or depleted states where the listener may benefit from a carefully structured ascent into more engagement, motivation, and drive.
Explore Transforming protocols →
