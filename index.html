The Why Behind This

Built by someone who needed it. Free because you might need it too.

The Drum Protocols started with a personal struggle — a dysregulated nervous system that wasn't responding to the usual interventions. Not therapy. Not medication adjustments. Not mindfulness apps. Something was missing at the level of the body's own rhythmic processing.

The research pointed to entrainment — the auditory brainstem's ability to synchronise with a periodic rhythmic signal. But nothing existed that was systematically designed around that mechanism, built to be comparable across protocols, or validated by real listener data.

So it was built from scratch. Thirty protocols across three physiological directions. Each one grounded in the same φ-derived mathematical constant so they can be directly compared. An anonymous feedback system so the community shapes what works.

This is not a product. There is nothing to buy. Every protocol, every insight, every piece of data is free — because the people who need nervous system support the most are often the least resourced to pay for it.
